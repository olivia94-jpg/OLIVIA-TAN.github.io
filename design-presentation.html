為什麼製作這個應用

多數 AIGC 產品把「對話」塞進高度同質的聊天視窗：白底或暗色、氣泡列表、缺少空間感與情緒層次。對從事視覺傳達與創意發想的設計師而言，靈感往往短暫、碎片化，卻又需要被温柔地接住、結構化地延展。

我們想回答一個設計命題：若 AI 是創作夥伴，它的「容器」是否也應該承載創造力本身？ 星空靈感以「可成長的星空」取代單調對話框，讓記錄與回顧靈感成為一場有儀式感、可沉浸的體驗，而不只是另一份聊天記錄。

English

Why we built this

Most AIGC products trap “conversation” inside look‑alike chat shells — light or dark, bubble lists, little sense of space or emotional depth. For designers in visual communication and ideation, sparks are often fleeting and fragmented, yet need to be held gently and extended into structure.

We posed one design question: If AI is a creative partner, shouldn’t its container carry creativity too? Starfield Inspiration replaces the flat chat box with a growing night sky, so capturing and revisiting ideas feels immersive and ritual — not just another chat log.

在視覺傳達設計流程中的使用

  • 發想與捕捉：以語音快速說出腦中畫面、關鍵字或情緒，避免打字打斷心流；適合靈感突現、手邊不便打字的情境。
  • 釐清與結構：透過 AI 的提問與回應，把模糊概念逐步收斂成可執行的方向（受眾、語氣、媒介、限制條件等）。
  • 視覺化「進度感」：每次來回在星空中留下連線與節點，讓抽象的思考過程具備可見的時間軸與關聯，接近設計師熟悉的「牆上貼滿草圖與連線」的工作方式。
  • 歸檔與回看：結束一輪對話後生成結構化卡片（標題、時間、摘要、行動），搭配搜尋與星空高亮，支援專案中後期的回溯與提案準備。

English

Use in the visual communication workflow

  • Capture: Speak images, keywords, or mood by voice to avoid breaking flow — ideal when ideas appear suddenly or typing is awkward.
  • Clarify & structure: Through AI prompts and replies, fuzzy concepts become actionable (audience, tone, medium, constraints).
  • Visible progress: Each turn leaves links and nodes in the sky, giving abstract thinking a visible timeline and relationships — closer to a wall of sketches and threads.
  • Archive & revisit: Ending a session yields structured cards (title, time, summary, actions); search highlights stars in the scene for late‑project reviews and presentations.

設計思維過程

  • 同理與定義：從設計師日常痛點出發——靈感易逝、工具介面冰冷、歷史靈感難以美感化整理——收斂成一句問題：如何讓「與 AI 共思」變得願意停留、容易回來？
  • 概念隱喻：選擇「星空／星座」作為核心隱喻：星星＝靈感節點，連線＝思路關聯，夜空＝專注與沉澱的情緒場域。
  • 體驗原型：先確立全螢幕粒子場景、底部輸入與「結束對話→靈感星」的主路徑，再疊加搜尋、卡片、多語與可調視覺參數，反覆調整可讀性與沉浸感的平衡。
  • 驗證直覺：以「是否減少機械感、是否強化儀式與記憶點」作為主觀可用性指標，迭代動畫節奏與介面層級。

English

Design thinking process

  • Empathize & define: From designer pain — fleeting sparks, cold UIs, messy history — we asked how co‑thinking with AI could feel worth staying in and easy to return to.
  • Metaphor: Night sky / constellations — stars as idea nodes, lines as links, darkness as a calm emotional field.
  • Prototype: Full‑screen particles, bottom input, and “end session → inspiration star” first; then search, cards, i18n, tunable visuals — iterating legibility vs. immersion.
  • Sense‑check: Subjective criteria — less mechanical feel, stronger ritual and memory — guided motion pacing and UI hierarchy.

關鍵 UI／UX 決策（一）

全螢幕星空場景，而非置中聊天面板

決策：主畫面由 Three.js 粒子星空佔滿視野，訊息以輕量「沉浸訊息流」浮於下緣，而非傳統左右氣泡欄。

原因：視覺傳達工作者對場景、氛圍與層次敏感；邊界清楚的聊天框會暗示「事務性對答」。全螢幕場景把使用者放在「創作空間」裡，降低與一般聊天產品的聯想，並為後續連線、高亮、光球儀式預留同一套空間邏輯。

English

Key UI/UX decision (1): Full‑screen starfield, not a centered chat panel

Decision: Three.js particles fill the view; messages appear as a light immersive stream along the bottom, not classic L/R bubbles.

Why: Visual communicators read scene, mood, and depth; a boxed chat frame signals transactional Q&A. The full canvas places people in a “studio” and sets one spatial language for links, highlights, and the orb ritual.

關鍵 UI／UX 決策（二）

語音與文字並行，強調低打斷

決策：麥克風與文字欄同列於底欄，語音可作為主路徑之一。

原因：發想階段常伴隨走動、畫草圖或盯著畫面；語音減少視線在鍵盤與靈感之間切換的成本。底欄固定位置符合單手與大螢幕操作習慣，避免浮動按鈕遮擋星空主體。

English

Key UI/UX decision (2): Voice + text, low interruption

Decision: Mic and text field share one bottom bar; voice can be the primary path.

Why: Ideation often happens while moving, sketching, or staring at the canvas — voice cuts gaze cost between keyboard and idea. A fixed bar suits one‑hand and large displays and avoids FABs covering the sky.

關鍵 UI／UX 決策（三）

星座連線＋「靈感星」投擲儀式

決策：對話過程在星空中累積連線；按下「結束對話」時，以光球動畫將本輪對話收束為一顆持久節點。

原因：設計師需要段落感——知道這一輪思考已被「封存」。動畫不只是裝飾，而是狀態轉換的確認回饋（類似存檔、合版、收筆），有助建立情緒上的滿足與記憶錨點。

English

Key UI/UX decision (3): Constellation lines + “inspiration star” ritual

Decision: Dialogue adds links in the sky; “end session” plays an orb that settles into a lasting node.

Why: Designers need closure — a sense this round of thinking is sealed. Motion is state-change feedback (like save, merge, or signing a sketch), building satisfaction and a memorable anchor.

關鍵 UI／UX 決策（四）

靈感卡片、搜尋與星空高亮

決策：每輪對話產出帶標題、時間、摘要與行動的卡片；頂部搜尋可定位歷史靈感，並在星空中對應高亮。

原因：純文字紀錄不利快速掃視與向他人展示；卡片化對齊設計師熟悉的mood board／專案條目心智模型。搜尋 + 空間高亮把「找資料」從捲動長列表，變成在記憶地圖上定位，強化工具與視覺思維的一致性。

English

Key UI/UX decision (4): Cards, search, sky highlights

Decision: Each session yields a card (title, time, summary, actions); top search locates history and highlights matching stars.

Why: Plain threads are hard to scan or present; cards match mood‑board / project‑entry mental models. Search + spatial highlight turns “find a file” into pinning a memory on a map — aligned with visual thinking.

關鍵 UI／UX 決策（五）

設定收合、粒子形態與後製參數

決策：進階選項（API、粒子形態、Bloom、殘影等）收進側欄面板，預設不干擾主創作流。

原因：專業使用者需要可調的視覺語彙以配合品牌或心境，但初學者不能被滑桿淹沒。分層資訊架構維持「預設即能用、需要時再深入」。

English

Key UI/UX decision (5): Collapsed settings, shapes, post‑FX

Decision: Advanced options (API, particle shapes, bloom, afterimage, etc.) live in a side panel and stay out of the default flow.

Why: Power users need a tunable visual language for brand or mood; beginners must not drown in sliders. Layered IA keeps “works by default, depth on demand.”

開發過程（簡述）

實作上以 Three.js 建構粒子與連線，GSAP 輔助節奏感較強的過場；對話層與星空層解耦，方便日後更換模型供應商。介面字串與產品敘事支援中／英切換，便於展示與協作。

簡報重點置於設計意圖與體驗決策；技術棧為達成上述體驗的手段，而非目的本身。

English

Implementation (brief)

Three.js drives particles and lines; GSAP handles punchier transitions. Chat and sky are decoupled so the model provider can change. UI copy and narrative support Chinese / English for demos and collaboration.

This deck emphasizes design intent and UX choices; the stack is a means to that experience, not the goal.

結語

把靈感交給一片會記住你的星空

星空靈感嘗試證明：同一套語言模型能力，放在不同的空間隱喻、儀式與資訊架構中，會帶來截然不同的創作感受。設計師值得擁有不僅「好用」、而且願意久留的 AI 共創環境。
